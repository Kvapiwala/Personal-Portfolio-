About Me

Hi, My name is Kush Vapiwala, I am 14 years old and live in Dublin, CA. I am currently an Emerald High School Freshman, part of the graduating class of 2027.

Racecar driver, police officer, astronaut, doctor, these are all career options we have looked up to and ranted about as children. However, as I continue to grow up, I have a desire to be a software engineer in the field of computer science. I find software engineering really interesting and I just love coding in general, I love the process of writing code, testing, making changes, till you get a finished product. The sense of achievement after every failed attempt is just a great feeling to hold.
